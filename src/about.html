About Us

We are a tech company which offers web development, graphic design, AI refactoring, CMS and CRM system setup, SEO, and marketing services.

The Color in Code adventure began with just a single member at the age of 17 who began to dive into programming as a hobby with no formal college, code camp, or mentorship. Within a short few years this had quickly morphed into a freelance business.

Color in Code has been offering websites, graphic design, and much more for over 15 years. One thousand six-hundred projects, and several fortune 500 clients later, we still love it. As our team continues to grow, we continue to discover new things to be passionate about in the world of programming.
